Bryan Ko

Multi Media Digital Artist

Bryan Ko, or by his artist name, Rya Sprouts or Sprouttess, is an Asian American digital artist from the Bay Area. He graduated from San Jose State University with a Bachelor's of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art. He first started his art journey in high school, learning photography and videography. Upon his arrival to San Jose State University, he’s learned new skills such as 3D modeling coding, and animation. Through his education, he's been further enhancing his skills in video editing and motion graphics.

To learn more about my work history, check out my resume. Thanks for stopping by!
