Exhibitions

CTRL_Dystopia, 2025

  • Hammer Theatre, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA

    • One of the 21 DMA artists that presented their work at Hammer Theater on Nov 15, 2025. CTRL_Dystopia was the BFA show for the graduating DMA students.

    Nexus, 2025

  • Art & Design Building, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA

    • One of the artists from a group of friends that presented their work in the Gale Antokal gallery. The show highlights the friend group that formed over their time at SJSU. The show was up from OCT 28th - 30th.

    Lightboxes by CADRE S.O., 2025

  • Art & Design Building, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA
  • Maker Faire Vallejo, Vallejo, CA

    • One of 2 artworks from the SJSU CADRE S.O. club. Each lightbox design corresponds to what each letter stands for.

    Collective Bloom by CADRE S.O., 2025

  • Art & Design Building, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA
  • Maker Faire Vallejo, Vallejo, CA

    • One of 2 artworks from the SJSU CADRE S.O. club. Members of the club and students created many different and unique flowers that were later on displayed using projection mapping.

    Admitted Spartan Day (CADRE S.O. Showcase), 2025

  • Art & Design Building, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA

    • One of the artist that showed off their artwork to new SJSU students and families, while also showing what the DMA program has to offer.

    Medals of Dishonor, 2024

  • Art & Design Building, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA

    • One of the art students that showed off their medal of dishonor design. Opposite to the medal of honor where you honor a soldier, a medal of dishonor is a satirical piece that is anti-something or against an idea.
